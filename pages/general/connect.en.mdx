## {getLocale("pagesDescription", "en")}

Adds your Steam account to your Discord account and shares this information with Civ6Bot. When you call the command, a message appears visible only to you, with an authorization button. 
When you click the button, it takes you to a special authorization page on this website. 
Upon success, you can use the command to [send a link to the game lobby](./link).

<Callout emoji="⚠️">
    Sometimes you can't connect your Steam account. If you are sure that you did everything correctly, then please notify the bot developer about this.
</Callout>

## Requirements
Before connecting your Steam account, make sure you meet the following requirements:
- your steam account connected to [Discord Connections ↗](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/8063233404823-Connections-Linked-Roles-Community-Members);
- your Steam profile [is visible to Public ↗](https://help.steampowered.com/en/faqs/view/588C-C67D-0251-C276) (is not private).
